How to Create Your Influencer Profile (Free Tool)

An influencer profile is your professional snapshot for brands — it combines your platforms, audience, content, and results in one place. This guide shows you what to include and how to build a modern influencer profile using InfluenceHub.

What Is an Influencer Profile?

An influencer profile is a public webpage that showcases a creator's platforms, audience metrics, brand collaborations, niche, and contact details. It's a modern replacement for traditional media kits and lets brands evaluate creators instantly.

Unlike static PDFs or scattered social media links, an influencer profile brings everything together in one shareable link. Brands can quickly see your reach, engagement, content style, and past work without downloading files or navigating multiple platforms.

Think of it as your digital business card, portfolio, and media kit combined — always up to date and accessible from any device.

Why Your Influencer Profile Matters

  • Brand trust: A professional profile shows you take your creator career seriously and makes brands more likely to work with you.
  • Professionalism: Stand out from creators who only share screenshots or basic links in DMs.
  • Faster decision-making: Brands can evaluate you in seconds instead of piecing together information from multiple sources.
  • Replacing clunky PDFs: No more emailing large files or worrying about outdated media kits. Your profile updates in real time.

How to Create Your Influencer Profile with InfluenceHub

  1. Sign up on InfluenceHub. Create a free account at app.influencehub.net/signup using your email or social login.
  2. Add your name, niche, and About Me. Start with the basics: your creator name, what you create content about, and a brief bio that explains who you are and what makes your content unique.
  3. Connect or manually add your social platforms and follower counts. Add your TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, or other platforms. Include current follower counts and key engagement metrics if available.
  4. Highlight a few top pieces of content or brand collaborations. Showcase your best work — whether it's viral videos, successful brand partnerships, or content that represents your style.
  5. Add your contact info and preferred collab types. Let brands know how to reach you and what kinds of partnerships you're interested in (product reviews, sponsored posts, long-term ambassadorships, etc.).
  6. Share your InfluenceHub link in DMs, emails, and your bio. Once your profile is ready, use your unique InfluenceHub link everywhere you pitch brands or showcase your work.
